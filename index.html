Welcome to SportHawk

SportHawk is an innovative networking and organisational app designed to help grassroots sports clubs streamline their operations and facilitate team growth.

SportHawk Features

Find and join new clubs on our map, based on your interests

Become part of team and find a new sense of belonging amongst your community

Stay organised with all the newest and most efficient team management tools

Engage with all the latest local sports news and results across the country

"It is my mission to increase accessibility to local community and address the rising physical, mental, and social health issues present in our society today. SportHawk is striving to make a positive impact across sporting communities through the power of connection and sport."

- Ashton Sims, Founder of SportHawk
